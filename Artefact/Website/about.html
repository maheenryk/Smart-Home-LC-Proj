What do we do?

We make your home smarter than ever before. With our exceptionally priced plans you will get to not only control the lights in each room of your house, but the air conditioning, the windows, even the blinds. Along with tremendous control, view the temperatures throughout the house in a visual graph presented in your home page. Once logged in, you will be able to use our website to control your house.

Allow your home to become responsive to the environment within. No need to worry about coming home to a cold or hot house, keep it perfect at your desired temperature. When the temperature reaches a certain level our smart home device will automatically switch on the air conditioning. You will be able to monitor the changes on the website and turn the air conditioner off if you wish.
